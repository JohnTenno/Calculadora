What is the LPD

The Loli Police Department is a VRChat roleplaying community focused on the interaction with members of the public and the VRChat community at large. The LPD is an organization that is focused on its core beliefs of entertainment of the general populous, fighting against annoying memes, and protecting players from harassment. Community as a whole is an essential part of the everyday activity within the LPD and something that we take pride in.

The Loli Police Department is a group born out of a purely fun idea that former Chief Sarma had in 2017. After some tinkering with avatar works and worldmaking, this group became its entity in the year of 2018. Because of internal affairs, the original group ceased to exist in an event we refer to as "The Nuke", generating an environment for passionate members to help rebuild Sarma's legacy. These events lead to the administrations of people like Rickity, Hroi, Molls, and Karet to lead our group into what you currently experience at the Loli Police Department.
